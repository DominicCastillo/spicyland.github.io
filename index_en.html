Our History

How was SpicyLand born?

One day, a group of young visionaries, driven by the ambition to learn and grow in the field business, made the bold decision to embark on an exciting entrepreneurial project. With the determination To test their abilities and skills in managing finances, they decided to immerse themselves in the world of spicy sweets.

With a clear focus on customer satisfaction, they launched an exhaustive study of market to discover its star product: spicy sweets. This choice would not only allow them differentiate themselves in the market, but also challenge themselves and bring a unique experience to their consumers.

As a team they embarked on the strategic distribution of their products throughout the municipality of Saint Savior. Every step they took was full of enthusiasm and determination, facing challenges with a positive and proactive mindset.
